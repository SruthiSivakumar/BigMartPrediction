BIG MART - SALES PREDICTION¶

PROBLEM DESCRIPTION:¶

The dataset belongs to the retail chain.it contains information regarding the outlet wise data of the items that are sold. It captures the daily sales, and other attributes that affect the salesprice are tracked in the dataset. Our objective is to predict the sales of every item based on the provided characteristics of the items and the outlets

Item_Identifier - Uniquely identifies a product (categorical

UNDERSTANDING THE DATA¶

The dimensions of the dataset is 8523 Observations and 13 variables. It has 11 independent variables and 1 dependent variable which is Item Outlet Sales. Among the 11 IVs, 6 are factors and 5 are numerical. Two of the factors are the Identifiers - outlet_identifier and item_identifier which can be removed during model building as this data will not render any useful information

MISSING VALUE ANALYSIS¶

2 of the variables have missing values. Item-weight with 17.17 % of NAs The blank for the Item_weights are for the "Outlet_Type" - Supermarket_Market_3 and Tier 3 Outlet Size has 28.2 % of blanks - No blanks in Tier 1 and ST2 and ST3 blanks are there only in years 1998,2002,2007
